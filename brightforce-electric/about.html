Meet the Founder

From the Jobsite to Your Home

Marcus Delvecchio grew up in a small town outside Flagstaff, Arizona, where his father ran a small construction company. By age 16, Marcus was already helping run wire on job sites after school. What started as summer work turned into a true passion for the craft.

After graduating from the IBEW Apprenticeship Program at the top of his class in 2013, Marcus spent several years working commercial electrical jobs throughout the Phoenix metro area — office buildings, retail centers, and high-rise developments. But it was the residential side of the work he loved most: the chance to sit down with a homeowner, understand their needs, and solve real problems in the place they call home.

In 2014, Marcus took the leap and founded Bright Force Electric. His vision was simple: an electrical company that treated every homeowner the way he'd want his own family treated — with honesty, transparency, and craftsmanship that would last decades.

Today, Bright Force Electric has grown to a team of six licensed electricians and has completed over 1,200 residential jobs across Maricopa County. But Marcus still shows up on complex jobs personally, and he personally reviews every quote before it goes out the door.

"I built this company on one principle: do the job right the first time, and charge a fair price for it. Everything else follows from that."
