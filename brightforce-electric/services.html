Panel Upgrades & Replacements

An undersized or aging electrical panel is one of the most common — and dangerous — electrical issues in older homes. If your breakers trip frequently, you're planning a kitchen remodel, or you're adding high-draw appliances like an EV charger or hot tub, a panel upgrade is often required. We upgrade from 100A to 200A (or 400A) service panels quickly and safely, and handle all permits and utility coordination so you don't have to.

  • 100A → 200A Upgrades
  • 200A → 400A Upgrades
  • Federal Pacific Replacements
  • Sub-Panel Installation

Ceiling Fan Installation

A properly installed ceiling fan can reduce cooling costs by up to 40% in Arizona summers. We install ceiling fans of any size, weight, or style — including heavy-duty fans that require brace-mounted ceiling boxes, and fans in vaulted-ceiling rooms that need extension rods. All wiring is done neatly inside the ceiling with proper switch or remote control integration. We'll also dispose of your old fan at no extra charge.

  • Standard & Heavy-Duty Fans
  • Vaulted & Angled Ceilings
  • Remote & Smart Controls
  • Old Fan Removal & Disposal

EV Charger Installation

Charging your electric vehicle at home is faster, safer, and more convenient with a dedicated Level 2 charger. We install 240V EVSE (Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment) for all major EV brands including Tesla, Ford, GM, Rivian, and more. Our team handles the full process — from running the new circuit and installing the charger unit, to pulling the required permits and ensuring your installation is utility-compliant and future-ready.
