Drain Cleaning

Slow or completely blocked drains are one of the most common plumbing problems homeowners face. Johnson's Plumbing uses professional-grade equipment to clear clogs in kitchen sinks, bathroom drains, floor drains, and main sewer lines. We don't just punch through the clog — we fully clear the line and inspect it to make sure the problem is gone for good. No drain is too stubborn.

  • Kitchen & Bathroom Drains
  • Main Sewer Line Clearing
  • Floor Drain Cleaning
  • Hydro-Jetting Available

Pipe Repair & Replacement

Leaking, cracked, or burst pipes can cause serious water damage if left unaddressed. Our team quickly locates and repairs damaged pipes — whether it's a pinhole leak in a copper line, a cracked PVC fitting, or a frozen pipe that burst during a Utah winter. We handle full pipe replacements and repiping projects for older homes with corroded or failing plumbing systems.

  • Burst & Frozen Pipe Repair
  • Copper & PVC Repair
  • Full Repiping
  • Water Line Replacement

Water Heater Installation & Repair

Whether your water heater has stopped working, is running out of hot water too quickly, or is simply aging out, Johnson's Plumbing can help. We install, repair, and replace traditional tank water heaters and tankless (on-demand) water heaters from all major brands. We'll help you choose the right unit for your home's size and hot water demands, and handle the full installation including any required code upgrades.

  • Tankless Water Heater Installation

Sewer Line Repair & Replacement

A damaged or deteriorating sewer line can cause sewage backups, foul odors, and extensive property damage. We perform camera inspections to pinpoint the exact location and nature of the problem before any digging begins. Depending on the condition and location of the damage, we offer traditional excavation repairs, spot repairs, and trenchless repair methods to restore your sewer line with minimal disruption to your yard.

  • Sewer Camera Inspection
  • Sewer Line Repair
  • Full Sewer Replacement
  • Trenchless Options Available

Leak Detection

Hidden leaks behind walls, under slabs, or below your yard can waste thousands of gallons of water and cause significant structural damage before you even notice them. Using advanced leak detection equipment, we locate leaks accurately and non-invasively — so we're not tearing up your home searching blindly. Once found, we provide a clear repair plan and get it fixed fast.

  • Underground Water Line Leaks
